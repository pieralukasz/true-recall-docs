## Why True Recall?

True Recall transforms your Obsidian vault into an intelligent learning system. Create flashcards with AI, review them with state-of-the-art spaced repetition, and track your progress with comprehensive analytics.

## Quick Start

1. **Install** the plugin in Obsidian
2. **Configure** your OpenRouter API key
3. **Generate** flashcards from any note
4. **Review** with spaced repetition
5. **Track** your progress
