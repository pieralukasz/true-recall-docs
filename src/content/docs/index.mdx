## Features

Transform your Obsidian vault into an intelligent learning system. Create flashcards in a dedicated panel or directly in your notes, review them with state-of-the-art spaced repetition, and track your progress with comprehensive analytics.

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="FSRS v6 Algorithm" icon="setting">
		State-of-the-art spaced repetition with 21 trainable parameters.
		Unlike Anki, your flashcards live inside your notes — source and knowledge unified.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Local-First Storage" icon="document">
		All data stored in portable SQLite database within your vault.
		Your data stays with you, always.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Comprehensive Analytics" icon="seti:graphql">
		Track retention, forecast workload, and visualize progress with
		interactive charts. Query your learning data with natural language.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Projects System" icon="list-format">
		Organize flashcards into projects spanning multiple notes.
		Review cards by topic or subject area.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Cloze Deletions & Reversed Cards" icon="pencil">
		Create cloze deletions and reversed cards directly in your notes
		using simple tags.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Cloud Sync" icon="rocket">
		Cross-device synchronization via True Recall Cloud.
		Study on any device with automatic conflict resolution.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
